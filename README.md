# 💣 Bomb Chip Challenge (2-4 Players)

A fun multiplayer Roblox game where strategy meets suspense!
Secretly plant bombs in your opponent's chips, then take turns
eating — will you survive or go BOOM?

## 🎮 Gameplay
- 2 to 4 players supported
- Each player secretly places 3 bombs in opponent's chip zone
- Take turns picking and eating chips from the board
- Hit a bomb = lose a life 💥 (funny reaction!)
- Lose all 3 lives = full explosion 💀 game over
- Last player standing wins! 🏆

## ⏱️ Game Phases
- **Phase 1 (15 sec):** Secretly place your bombs
- **Phase 2:** Take turns eating — safe or boom!
